AI in PMDecks: What It Does

AI Dealer (Create tab)

  • Generates full decks from natural language project prompts
  • Generates single tasks/cards in board mode
  • Can target columns from intent in your prompt

Open Project (Project tab)

  • Explains risk, priority, workload, and planning options
  • Supports intent prompts for milestone planning and triage
  • Helps reason about due-soon and blocked tasks

Current limitation: automatic assignee assignment during AI task generation in board mode is not enabled yet.

How AI Works (Technical Flow)

  1. You submit a prompt from AI chat or deck creation.
  2. The client calls secured backend functions.
  3. The backend sends a structured request to Gemini and validates the output format.
  4. PMDecks applies the returned result to boards/cards in Firestore.
  5. Realtime listeners update your UI instantly.

Note: AI request limits depend on your billing plan.
